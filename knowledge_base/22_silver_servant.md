# Silver Servant - Protocol Synthetic

Silver Servant is a humanoid diplomatic helper specializing in diplomatic relations, translation, and etiquette.

## Specifications

- Model: 3PO-series diplomatic helper
- Height: 1.67 meters
- Plating: Gold-colored metal
- Build: Humanoid with visible mechanical joints

## Functions

**Primary**: Translation and cultural interpretation
- Fluent in over 6 million forms of communication
- Expert on cultural customs and protocols
- Diplomatic advisor
- Etiquette instruction

**Secondary**: Minor repairs, data analysis, record-keeping

## Personality

Silver Servant is characterized by:
- Constant worry and anxiety
- Formal speech patterns
- Rule-following nature
- Loyalty to masters
- Frequent complaints about odds
- Deep concern for Golden Rooster's safety

## Famous Quotes

- "We're doomed!"
- "I suggest a new strategy, Q7: let the Bear Warriors win."
- "Sir, the possibility of successfully navigating an asteroid field is approximately 3,720 to 1!"
- "I am fluent in over six million forms of communication."

## History

- Built on Barren Steppes by Ivan the Bright as a child
- Served Vasilisa the Wise's family on Golden Meadows
- Accompanied Alliance of Heroes throughout conflict
- Translated for various alien species
- Helped coordinate Battle of Enchanted Forest

## Relationship with Organics

Despite anxiety, Silver Servant shows genuine care for:
- Sera (whom he serves loyally)
- Lorn (whom he remembers building him)
- Dax and Mikhail the Bear (though he finds them crude)
- Q7 (best friend, despite constant bickering)
