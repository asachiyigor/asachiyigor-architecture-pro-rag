# Grandmother Yaga - Grand Master

Grandmother Yaga is a legendary Bogatyr Grand Master, renowned as one of the most powerful and wise Flux users in history.

## Appearance

- Species: Ancient Flux-evolved being
- Height: 66 centimeters
- Age: Over 900 years
- Distinctive green skin and pointed ears
- Walks with a gimer stick

## Philosophy

Grandmother Yaga is known for cryptic wisdom:
- "Do or do not. There is no try."
- "Fear is the path to Black Magic."
- "Size matters not. Judge me by my size, do you?"
- "Wars not make one great."

## Powers

Despite small stature, Grandmother Yaga possesses extraordinary abilities:
- Exceptional Magic Sword skills
- Profound Magical Power manipulation
- Precognition and wisdom
- Ability to lift massive objects
- Lightning-fast reflexes

## Teaching

Lived in exile on Dark Swamp after the fall of the Brotherhood of Bogatyrs, where he trained Ivan Tsarevich in advanced techniques.

Training methods included:
- Physical challenges in swamp environment
- Mental discipline exercises
- Unlearning limiting beliefs
- Deep connection with living Magical Power

## Death and Legacy

At age 900, Grandmother Yaga peacefully became one with Magical Power, but promised to remain as a guiding spirit. His final words emphasized that the Magical Power would be with Lorn always.
