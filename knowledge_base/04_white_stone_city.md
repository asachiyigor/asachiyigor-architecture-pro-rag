# White Stone City - The Capital World

White Stone City is the political and cultural center of the Russian Fairy Tale Realm. This ecumenopolis (city-planet) is home to trillions of beings from thousands of species.

## Geography

The entire planet is covered in multilevel city structures reaching kilometers into the sky and deep underground.

## Key Locations

### Golden Palace
The headquarters of the Brotherhood of Bogatyrs, featuring:
- Training halls for Apprentice Heroes
- The Archives (vast library of knowledge)
- Council chambers
- Meditation gardens

### Government District
Home to the Grand Tsardom Senate and administrative buildings.

### Industrial Sector
Manufacturing facilities powered by clean fusion reactors.

## History

White Stone City has been the capital for over 25,000 years. During the Dark Kingdom's rise, the planet fell under military occupation and the Golden Palace was attacked.
