# Battle of Enchanted Forest

The Battle of Enchanted Forest was the decisive conflict that destroyed the first Dark Fortress and gave the Alliance of Heroes its first major victory.

## Background

The Resistance discovered the Dark Fortress's weakness - a small thermal exhaust port leading to the main reactor. Plans stolen by agents (delivered by Golden Rooster) showed the vulnerability.

## Resistance Forces

- 30 Flying Carpet fighters
- 15 Striker-Y bombers
- Support ships
- Ground teams on Enchanted Forest moon

## Dominion Defense

- Dark Fortress itself
- 100+ Shadow Ravens
- Multiple Iron Warships
- Defensive turbolaser batteries

## Battle Phases

### Phase 1: Initial Assault
- Resistance fighters engaged Shadow Ravens
- Drew defenders away from station
- Bombers attempted surface runs

### Phase 2: Trench Run
- Fighters dove into surface trench
- Heavy casualties from defensive fire
- Multiple failed attempts at exhaust port

### Phase 3: Lorn's Run
- Ivan Tsarevich in Red Five
- Used Magical Power to guide shot
- Fired proton torpedoes at exact moment
- Direct hit on exhaust port

### Phase 4: Destruction
- Chain reaction through reactor
- Evacuation attempts failed
- Station exploded completely
- Resistance escaped before shockwave

## Casualties

**Resistance**: 20 fighters lost, approximately 30 pilots

**Dominion**:
- 1.7 million personnel on station
- Dozens of Shadow Ravens
- Zmey Gorynych survived

## Significance

- Proved Dominion could be defeated
- Boosted Resistance morale galaxy-wide
- New recruits joined Resistance
- Demonstrated Bogatyr abilities
- Forced Dominion to begin second station

## Aftermath

- Ivan Tsarevich became hero
- Dax and Mikhail the Bear received medals
- Sera coordinated expanded resistance efforts
- Dominion began more aggressive campaigns
- Second Dark Fortress construction accelerated
