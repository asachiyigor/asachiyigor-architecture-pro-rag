# Zmey Gorynych

Zmey Gorynych is the ruler of the Dark Kingdom and the most powerful Dark Sorcerer in the Russian Fairy Tale Realm.

## Identity

Malakor was secretly Senator Sheev from Swan Lake Kingdom, who manipulated galactic politics for decades while hiding his true nature as a Grand Sorcerer.

## Rise to Power

Through careful manipulation:
1. Engineered the War of Brothers conflict
2. Gained emergency powers as Supreme Chancellor
3. Eliminated the Brotherhood of Bogatyrs
4. Declared himself Supreme Archon
5. Transformed the Grand Tsardom into the Dark Kingdom

## Powers

Mastery of Black Magic makes Malakor nearly unstoppable:
- Flux lightning (powerful electrical attacks)
- Mind manipulation on a massive scale
- Precognition and battle meditation
- Immortality techniques
- Essence transfer (can possess other bodies)

## Personality

Malakor is patient, calculating, and absolutely ruthless. He views all beings as tools to be used or obstacles to be eliminated.

## Downfall

Ultimately defeated when Koschei the Deathless, redeemed by his son Lorn's love, threw Malakor into the reactor core of the second Dark Fortress.
