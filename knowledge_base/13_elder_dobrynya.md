# Elder Dobrynya - The Wise Master

Elder Dobrynya was a legendary Elder Bogatyr known for his wisdom and combat prowess.

## Early Years

Discovered as a young Magic-gifted child on the planet Stewjon, Theron was trained at the Golden Palace and eventually became one of the Order's most respected Masters.

## Accomplishments

- Defeated Dark Sorcerer Nightingale the Robber
- Trained Ivan the Bright (later Koschei the Deathless)
- Served on the Council of Wise Elders
- Negotiated peace in numerous conflicts
- Master of Magic Sword Form III (defensive)

## Philosophy

Theron believed in:
- Patience and understanding
- The primacy of knowledge over aggression
- Redemption for those who have fallen
- Living in harmony with Magical Power

## Sacrifice

When Order 66 was executed, Theron helped other Bogatyrs escape but was killed defending the Golden Palace. Before dying, he became one with Magical Power, learning to manifest as a spirit to guide future generations.

## Legacy

As a Magical Power spirit, Theron continued to mentor:
- Ivan Tsarevich in the ways of Bogatyrs
- Providing wisdom during critical battles
- Teaching the technique of immortality through Magical Power

His teachings emphasized that Magical Power is not just power, but understanding and compassion.
