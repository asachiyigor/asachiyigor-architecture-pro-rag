# Magical Power Overview

Magical Power is an energy field that permeates the entire Russian Fairy Tale Realm universe. It binds together all living things and gives Bogatyrs their extraordinary abilities.

## Properties

The Magical Power has two aspects:
- **White Magic**: Used by Bogatyrs for protection and knowledge
- **Black Magic**: Wielded by Dark Sorcerers for power and domination

## Abilities

Magic-gifted individuals can:
- Move objects with their mind
- Sense emotions and intentions
- Predict future events
- Enhance physical abilities
- Communicate telepathically
- Create energy shields

## Training

Young Magic-gifted individuals are taken to the Golden Palace on White Stone City to begin their training as Apprentice Heroes under a Elder Bogatyr.
