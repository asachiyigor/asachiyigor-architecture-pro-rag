# Brotherhood of Bogatyrs

The Brotherhood of Bogatyrs is an ancient organization of Magic-gifted peacekeepers who serve as guardians of justice throughout the Russian Fairy Tale Realm.

## History

Founded over 25,000 years ago, the Order has protected the galaxy through countless conflicts.

## Code

Bogatyrs follow a strict code:
- There is no emotion, there is peace
- There is no ignorance, there is knowledge
- There is no passion, there is serenity
- There is no chaos, there is harmony
- There is no death, there is Magical Power

## Structure

### Council of Wise Elders
Twelve Masters who lead the Order and make important decisions.

### Ranks
1. Youngling - Children being evaluated
2. Apprentice Hero - Student assigned to a Master
3. Young Bogatyr - Fully trained member
4. Elder Bogatyr - May train Apprentice Heroes
5. Council Member - Order leadership

## Training

Training begins in childhood and takes 15-20 years:
- Physical conditioning
- Magical Power techniques
- Philosophy and ethics
- Magic Sword combat
- Diplomacy and negotiation
- Galactic history and languages

## Fall

During the War of Brothers, Zmey Gorynych executed Order 66, commanding Enchanted Warriors to eliminate all Bogatyrs. Most were killed, with only a handful surviving in hiding.
