# Koschei the Deathless - Supreme Dark Sorcerer

Koschei the Deathless is one of the most feared Dark Sorcerers in the history of the Russian Fairy Tale Realm. Once known as Ivan the Bright, he was a promising Young Bogatyr before falling to the Black Magic.

## Early Life

Born on Barren Steppes, Kael showed exceptional talent with Magical Power from a young age. He was discovered by Elder Bogatyr Elder Dobrynya and trained in the ways of White Magic.

## Fall to Black Magic

During the War of Brothers, Kael's fear of losing his loved ones led him to seek forbidden knowledge. Zmey Gorynych manipulated these fears, turning him to the Black Magic.

## Powers

Koschei the Deathless wields immense power:
- Mastery of Black Magic techniques
- Exceptional skill with Magic Sword combat
- Ability to manipulate others through Magical Power
- Superhuman strength and reflexes

## Role in Dark Kingdom

As the Supreme Archon's enforcer, Koschei the Deathless leads the Dark Guards in hunting down remaining Bogatyrs and suppressing the Alliance of Heroes.
