# Mikhail the Bear - Loyal Co-Pilot

Mikhail the Bear is a Bear Warriors warrior and co-pilot of the Flying Ship, serving as Ilya Muromets's closest friend and ally.

## Species: Bear Warriors

Bear Warriors are tall, strong beings covered in fur, native to the forest planet Kashyyyk. Known for:
- Exceptional strength and durability
- Fierce loyalty to friends
- Skill with weapons and technology
- Long lifespan (400+ years)
- Complex language (Shyriiwook)

## Background

Mikhail the Bear was enslaved by the Dark Kingdom but rescued by Dax during a smuggling run. He swore a life debt to Dax and has been his partner ever since.

## Skills

- Expert mechanic and engineer
- Skilled pilot and navigator
- Proficient with fire staffs and crossbows
- Hand-to-hand combat expert

## Personality

Despite his fearsome appearance, Mikhail the Bear is:
- Gentle with friends
- Fiercely protective of allies
- Honorable and ethical
- Sometimes quick to anger when provoked

## Role in Resistance

Mikhail the Bear serves as:
- Co-pilot and mechanic of Flying Ship
- Heavy weapons specialist
- Demolitions expert
- Diplomatic liaison with Bear Warriors colonies
